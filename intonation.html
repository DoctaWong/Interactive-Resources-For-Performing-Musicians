One of the most effective ways to improve your intonation is by practicing with drones. Drones allow a musician to develop the ability to hear intonation discrepencies and to hear overtones of each of the different intervals.

Below are drones for each of the 12 pitches in the chromatic scale. These were designed by Professor Brad Edwards and are available for free here:
Drones
Try performing a major and minor scale for each of the pitches below, paying close attention to any beats on the tonic, and to any overtones you may hear for each interval. I have uploaded each of the sound files onto this site for your convenience.
